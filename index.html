Project Overview

We developed a neural compression system for driving video tokens that achieves significant compression while maintaining perfect reconstruction. The model combines convolutional layers for spatial understanding with bidirectional LSTM for temporal patterns, using a novel dual-output design that predicts both tokens and confidence scores.

Key Achievements

  • 3.2x compression ratio achieved
  • Perfect reconstruction maintained
  • 6 minute training time for 10 epochs

Technical Stack

  • PyTorch
  • CUDA-enabled GPU
  • Custom Dataset Management
  • Bidirectional LSTM
